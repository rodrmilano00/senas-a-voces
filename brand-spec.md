## Layout Posture
- Rounded corners: 16–24px (rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl)
- Generous gaps: 24–32px (gap-6, gap-8)
- Wave/organic curves inspired by logo book-waves
- Fluid transitions: cubic-bezier(0.23, 1, 0.32, 1) at 200ms
- One accent used per screen, maximum
- No shadows except interactive elevation
